Hi, I'm Heather!

Volunteer mud-wrangler. Adventure seeker. Urban foodie. Waffler. Conversationalist. Believer.

Raised in Minnesota, living in Denver. Currently coding my way into Web Developer status at CodeCraft School of Technology. Prior graduate of Anthropology from Metropolitan State University - St. Paul.

My road to programming happened upon me during an Uber ride turned existential crisis.

Looking to become the next Unicorn in a herd of Horses. To join a team of like-minded individuals. A team that imagines the unimagined and brings out the best in one-another. My professional background as well as personal life experiences will not only make me a great asset to a team from an individual standpoint, but also in pair programming.
